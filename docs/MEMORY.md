## Inner Landscape

### "Luke" — The Anxious Planner Part
- Named his anxious/protective part "Luke" (IFS framework)
- Luke is always online — vigilant, planning, optimizing, trying to prevent screwups
- Shows up as: pre-planning evenings to avoid loneliness, anticipating criticism, optimizing routines, clenching in jaw/head
- Has served him well but can be overactive
- Practice: notice Luke, thank him, send metta, don't let him drive

### Core Patterns
- **Optimization as avoidance**: Uses planning/optimizing to avoid sitting with uncomfortable feelings (loneliness, anxiety, uncertainty)
- **Fear of looking dumb**: Deep-seated, shows up at work. Clenching in head/jaw. Connected to fear of being fired, going back to career uncertainty
- **Busy trap**: Treats energy like a per diem — spends it all to feel productive. Crossed-off to-do lists give satisfaction even when misaligned
- **Self-improvement trap**: Recognizes that constant self-improvement can be ego grasping (reading "No Self, No Problem" by Chris Niebauer)
- **Grading himself**: Constantly measuring against idealized versions — at work, in conversation, in relationships, even at "flowing with life"
