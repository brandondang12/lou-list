## 🗓️ PROPOSED ITINERARY (10 Days)

### **Day 1: Thu June 26 — Arrive Bilbao**
**Fly:** LAX → Bilbao (likely 1 connection, arrive evening/next morning)  
**Note:** Most routes connect through Madrid, Barcelona, or European hub

**If arrive evening:**
- Pick up rental car at airport

- Early night to recover from travel

**Sleep:** Bilbao (Old Town area)

---

### **Day 2: Fri June 27 — Bilbao + Sacred Mountain**
**Morning:**
- Quick Guggenheim Museum visit (iconic building, even if not art-focused)
- OR skip museum, chill morning/coffee in Old Town

**Afternoon/Evening:**
- **MYSTICAL:** Drive to **Mount Anboto** (45 min from Bilbao)
- Hike to Mari's cave (the goddess's dwelling!)
- Moderate hike, ~3-4 hours round trip
- Stunning views, sacred site vibes

**Evening:**
- Return to Bilbao
- Pintxos in Casco Viejo (bar hop)
- Try traditional cider houses if open

**Sleep:** Bilbao

**Drive time today:** ~1.5 hours total

---

### **Day 3: Sat June 28 — Camino Norte: Bilbao → Gernika**
**Morning:**
- **CAMINO:** Walk a section of the Camino Norte coastal route
- Start in Portugalete (20 min from Bilbao)
- Walk to Pobeña beach (12-15 km, ~4 hours)
- Beautiful coastal cliffs, beach sections
- Arrange car pickup or taxi back

**Afternoon:**
- Drive to **Gernika** (30 min)
- Visit Tree of Gernika (sacred Basque oak)
- Monday Market if timing works (skip if not Monday)
- Explore town where Picasso's "Guernica" was inspired

**Evening:**
- Drive to **Mundaka** (15 min from Gernika)
- Surf session at Mundaka (world-class left-hand wave!)
- **Note:** June swell can be smaller, but check forecast

**Sleep:** Mundaka or nearby (Bermeo)

**Drive time today:** ~1 hour total (plus Camino walking)

---

### **Day 4: Sun June 29 — Surf + Coastal Villages**
**Morning:**
- Another surf session in Mundaka (if waves)
- OR surf Bakio beach (beginner-friendly, 15 min away)

**Afternoon:**
- Drive coastal route to **San Juan de Gaztelugatxe**
- Iconic island hermitage, stone bridge (Game of Thrones spot)
- Short hike, dramatic views
- **MYSTICAL:** Built on ancient pagan site

**Late Afternoon:**
- Explore **Elantxobe** or **Lekeitio** (tiny cliffside fishing villages)
- Authentic Basque coastal life
- Fresh seafood

**Evening:**
- Drive toward San Sebastián area
- Stop in **Zarautz** for evening surf (30 min from Lekeitio)
- Long beach break, beginner-friendly

**Sleep:** Zarautz or San Sebastián

**Drive time today:** ~1.5 hours total

---

### **Day 5: Mon June 30 — San Sebastián Day**
**Morning:**
- Sleep in, recover from surfing/hiking
- Coffee + pastry in San Sebastián Old Town

**Mid-Morning:**
- **HIKE:** Mount Ulia coastal trail
- Start from Old Town, 2-3 hours round trip
- Panoramic views of La Concha Bay
- Easy-moderate, beautiful

**Afternoon:**
- Beach time at La Concha or Zurriola (surf beach)
- Relax, swim, maybe light surf

**Evening:**
- **PINTXOS CRAWL** in Parte Vieja (Old Town)
- Bar hop: Gandarias, La Cuchara de San Telmo, Borda Berri, Txepetxa
- This is THE Basque experience
- Txikiteo (social bar hopping)

**Sleep:** San Sebastián

**Drive time today:** Minimal (walking day)

### **Day 6: Tue July 1 — Day Trip: Hondarribia + French Border**
**Morning:**
- Drive to **Hondarribia** (30 min)
- Medieval walled town, colorful Basque houses
- Walk the old town, harbor area

**Afternoon:**
- Cross into France (15 min)
- **MYSTICAL:** Drive to **Zugarramurdi Witches' Cave** (45 min from Hondarribia)
- Massive natural cave where "witches" allegedly gathered
- 1610 witch trials site
- Museum about Basque witchcraft/mythology
- Beautiful karst formations

**Late Afternoon:**
- Explore French Basque villages: Saint-Jean-de-Luz or Biarritz
- OR return to Spanish side

**Evening:**
- Return to San Sebastián
- Chill evening, maybe sunset at beach

**Sleep:** San Sebastián

**Drive time today:** ~2 hours total

## 🌊 SURF LOGISTICS

### **Spots on Route:**
1. **Mundaka** — world-class left, advanced (can be mellow in summer)
2. **Bakio** — beach break, beginner-friendly
3. **Zarautz** — long beach, consistent, all levels
4. **Zurriola (San Sebastián)** — city beach break

### **Gear:**
**Bring:** Wetsuit (3/2mm for June), booties if you have
**Rent:** Boards available at all surf towns (€15-25/day)

**June conditions:**
- Water temp: 60-65°F (wetsuit needed)
- Waves: Smaller than winter, but can score
- Check forecast: Magicseaweed or Surfline

## ⚡ QUICK REFERENCE

**Weather in late June:**
- Temps: 65-75°F (cooler than inland Spain)
- Some rain possible (bring light jacket)
- Long daylight (sunset ~9:30pm)

**Language:**
- Spanish works everywhere
- Basque (Euskara) on signs
- English in tourist areas (San Sebastián, Bilbao)

**Driving:**
- Right-hand side
- Tolls on some highways (have card/cash)
- Parking can be tight in old towns

**Tipping:**
- Not expected like US
- Round up or leave small amount if good service
