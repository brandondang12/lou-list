## 🏔️ WESTERN US

### **Ananda Village (California)**
**Vibe:** Spiritual community, yoga, meditation, intentional living  
**Location:** Nevada City, CA (Sierra foothills)  
**July Weather:** Warm to hot (80-95°F)  
**Visitor Info:** Retreat center, guest programs, "Expanding Light" retreat  
**Why:** Large, well-established spiritual community (since 1968). Beautiful mountain setting.  
**Notes:** Very accessible, near Nevada City (cool small town)

### **Twin Oaks Intentional Community (Virginia)**
**Vibe:** Income-sharing commune, egalitarian, tofu production  
**Location:** Louisa, Virginia (rural, between Charlottesville & Richmond)  
**July Weather:** Hot & humid (85-95°F)  
**Visitor Info:** 3-week visitor program (required for prospective members)  
**Why:** One of the longest-running communes in the US (since 1967). Very communal — shared income, governance.  
**Notes:** Serious commitment to egalitarian living. Not a retreat — it's a working community.

### **East Wind Community (Missouri)**
**Vibe:** Income-sharing commune, egalitarian (sibling community to Twin Oaks)  
**Location:** Tecumseh, Missouri (Ozarks)  
**July Weather:** Hot (85-95°F)  
**Visitor Info:** Visitor programs  
**Why:** Similar to Twin Oaks, beautiful Ozark setting  
**Notes:** More rural/isolated than Twin Oaks

### **Acorn Community Farm (Virginia)**
**Vibe:** Income-sharing, seed company, gardening focus  
**Location:** Mineral, Virginia (near Twin Oaks)  
**Visitor Info:** Visitor programs  
**Why:** Sister community to Twin Oaks, focuses on seed/gardening business  
**Notes:** Smaller, more garden-focused

### **The Farm (Tennessee)**
**Vibe:** Historic commune, eco-village, midwifery/wellness focus  
**Location:** Summertown, Tennessee  
**July Weather:** Hot & humid (85-95°F)  
**Visitor Info:** Tours, workshops, ecovillage training center  
**Why:** One of the most famous communes (founded 1971). Now more of an eco-village with private land ownership but strong community.  
**Notes:** Historic hippie commune, evolved into eco-village model

### **Dancing Rabbit Ecovillage (Missouri)**
**Vibe:** Radical sustainability, car-free, off-grid  
**Location:** Rutledge, Missouri (prairie)  
**July Weather:** Hot (85-95°F)  
**Visitor Info:** 1-3 week visitor programs  
**Why:** Most committed eco-village in US — ecological covenants, renewable energy, low-impact living  
**Notes:** Rustic! Serious eco-living immersion.

## 🔍 HOW TO VISIT

**Most communities require:**
1. **Advance booking** — Don't just show up
2. **Application/interview** — Even for short visits
3. **Contribution** — Work exchange, fees, or donations
4. **Orientation** — Learning community agreements/culture

**Typical visitor formats:**
- **Day tours** — Some offer guided tours
- **Weekend workshops** — Intro experiences
- **Week-long programs** — "Experience Week" type programs
- **1-3 week visitor programs** — Trial living
- **Volunteer/internships** — Extended stays (weeks to months)
