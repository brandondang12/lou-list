## 🎬 STORYTELLING FORMATS WITH EXAMPLES

### **Format 1: Short-Form Video Testimonial (60-90 sec)**

**Example:**
- Headspace Instagram Reels
- User talking to camera
- Key elements: problem → insight → change

**Template:**
```
"I used to [struggle]..."
"Rosebud helped me realize [insight]..."
"Now I [specific change]..."
```

**Platform:** Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts

---

### **Format 2: Written Story with Photos**

**Example:**
- Noom success stories page
- Structure: Where I was → Journey → Where I am now

**Key Elements:**
- Real name and photo (with permission)
- Specific details
- Quotes in user's own words
- 300-500 words

**Platform:** Blog, email newsletter, website

---

### **Format 3: Mini-Documentary (3-5 min)**

**Example:**
- Peloton member stories on YouTube
- Cinematic but shot in user's home

**Production:**
- User voiceover narration
- B-roll of daily life

**Example:**

- User creates content organically

## 📊 STORYTELLING FRAMEWORKS

### **Framework 1: The Insight Story**

**Best for:** Rosebud's AI-driven insights

**Structure:**
1. The pattern I didn't see
2. The question Rosebud asked
3. The realization
4. What changed

**Example:**
> "I didn't realize I was journaling about work stress every Sunday night. Rosebud asked me, 'What about Mondays makes you anxious?' That question made me realize I was dreading meetings with my manager, not the work itself. I scheduled a conversation with her, and now Sundays feel different."

**References:**
- Oura Ring stories (data reveals pattern)
- Noom stories (insight leads to change)

---

### **Framework 2: The Specific Challenge Story**

**Best for:** Targeting specific use cases

**Structure:**
1. My specific challenge (anxiety, grief, relationship, etc.)
2. What I tried before
3. How Rosebud was different
4. One thing that changed

**Example:**
> "After my dad died, I couldn't talk about it. I tried traditional journaling but stared at blank pages. Rosebud asked me gentle questions I could actually answer. Now I can look back and see my grief journey mapped out."

**References:**
- BetterHelp stories (specific mental health challenges)
- Headspace stories (specific use cases)

---

### **Framework 3: The Habit Formation Story**

**Best for:** Showing sustained engagement

**Structure:**
1. I never stuck with journaling before
2. Day 1 vs. Day 30 vs. Day 90
3. What made it stick this time
4. The unexpected benefit

**Example:**
> "I've tried journaling apps before and quit after a week. With Rosebud, I'm on day 127. The AI questions feel like a friend checking in. The unexpected benefit? I stopped therapy because I'm processing things in real-time now." (Note: Careful with "stopped therapy" claims)

**References:**
- Matt D'Avella 30-day challenges
- Duolingo streak stories

## ⚠️ ETHICAL GUIDELINES

### **Consent & Privacy:**
- ✅ Explicit permission to share
- ✅ Option for anonymity (first name only, no photo)
- ✅ Right to revoke consent anytime
- ✅ Clear about where story will be used
- ❌ Never share journal content without permission
- ❌ No pressure to participate

### **Mental Health Sensitivity:**
- ✅ Careful with clinical terms (depression, anxiety, etc.)
- ✅ Avoid "cured" or "fixed" language
- ✅ "Rosebud helped me understand..." not "Rosebud cured..."
- ✅ Ongoing journey language
- ❌ Don't position as replacement for therapy
- ❌ No triggering content without warnings

### **Authenticity:**
- ✅ Real people, real words
- ✅ Small edits for clarity only
- ✅ No exaggeration
- ✅ Show range of experiences
- ❌ No paid testimonials
- ❌ No cherry-picking only extreme stories
